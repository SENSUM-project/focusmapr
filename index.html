Focus your data collection

A Focus Maps is a raster representation of the spatial probability of sampling a geographical location based on the combination of spatial indicators.

Installation

Installing from the github repository is the simplest solution:

> install.packages('devtools')
> install_github("SENSUM-project/focusmapr")

several dependencies should be installed as well:

> install.packages('raster')
> install.packages('spatstat')
> install.packages('rgdal')
